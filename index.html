WORK

Diplom-Is is a well-known Norwegian ice cream company that has been delighting customers with frozen treats for decades. The company offers a wide range of ice cream products, from classic flavors to innovative new creations.

The logistics division at Diplom-Is has played a crucial role in maintaining the cold chain, ensuring that ice cream remains fresh and at the right temperature during transportation. With a dedicated fleet of delivery vehicles, the company has supplied retailers efficiently across Norway.

However, by the end of 2025, Diplom-Is will shut down its direct logistics operations and transition to a wholesaler-based distribution model. This means the company will no longer handle deliveries internally but instead rely on external distributors to manage the supply chain.

This decision will impact 109 employees currently working in logistics. Diplom-Is has stated that efforts will be made to support those affected and explore alternative employment opportunities.

The move aims to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and create a more sustainable distribution model for the future.
