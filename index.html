Welcome to My World

Hey there! I'm Tommy, a lifelong explorer of both digital and natural landscapes. At 43 (soon to be 44 in October!), I’ve spent decades diving deep into the gaming universe, where strategy, adventure, and a touch of competition fuel my enthusiasm.

This space is where my interests collide—where pixels meet pine trees and creativity thrives both online and off. Whether you're here to chat gaming, swap outdoor survival tips, or share ideas on crafting from found materials, you're in the right place!
